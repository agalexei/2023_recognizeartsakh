WHITEWASHING

OF CRIMES
BY AZERBAIJAN

There have been several examples of Azerbaijan attempting to downplay or deny reports of war crimes committed during the 2020 Nagorno-Karabakh conflict:
01. Disputing the use of cluster bombs:
Azerbaijan has been accused of using cluster bombs in heavily populated areas, resulting in civilian casualties. However, Azerbaijan has denied these allegations and claimed that they only used precision-guided weapons.
02. Rebranding military operations:
Azerbaijan has referred to its military operations as "counter-offensive" actions, rather than acknowledging the aggression towards Artsakh (Nagorno-Karabakh).
03. Censorship of evidence:
Azerbaijan has suppressed evidence of war crimes through censorship and intimidation of journalists and human rights organizations.
04. Blaming Armenia for civilian casualties:
Azerbaijan has sought to blame Armenia for civilian casualties, despite evidence of Azerbaijani forces targeting civilian areas.
These actions are a part of the larger narrative of Azerbaijan attempting to downplay its actions and portray itself as the victim in the conflict, rather than acknowledging its own responsibility for war crimes.
